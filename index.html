Son Heung-min (Korean: 손흥민; born 8 July 1992) is a South Korean professional footballer who plays as a winger or striker for Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur and captains the South Korea national team.[4] Widely considered one of the best wingers in the world and widely regarded to be one of three best Asian players in European football history, Son is often called an icon of South Korean culture,[6][7][8][9][10] and was nominated for the Ballon d'Or in 2019.[11]
